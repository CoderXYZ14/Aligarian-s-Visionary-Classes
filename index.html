FAQ
Common Questions
Here are some of the most common questions that we get.
What courses do you offer?
We offer comprehensive coaching for grades 6th, 9th, and 11th, covering various subjects along with specialized preparation for the AMU entrance exam. Our curriculum is designed to ensure a holistic learning experience.
Who are your instructors?
Our instructors are highly qualified professionals with extensive experience in teaching. They are experts in their respective fields and are dedicated to providing quality education and guidance to our students.
How do I enroll my child in your coaching classes?
Enrolling in our classes is easy! You can visit our website and follow the admission procedure outlined in the "Admissions" section. Alternatively, you can visit our center during office hours for in-person assistance.
Do you provide study materials for the AMU entrance exam?
Yes, we offer comprehensive study materials, including mock tests, past papers, and specialized content tailored for the AMU entrance exam. Our aim is to equip students with the necessary resources to excel in the exam.
How do you track students' progress?
We have regular assessments, periodic tests, and feedback mechanisms in place to monitor students' progress. This helps us identify areas needing improvement and provide necessary support to ensure overall development.
